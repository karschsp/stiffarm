About

‘Stiff Arm’ was performed, recorded and mixed by Snap Infraction (that's Tony and Steve for those of you keeping score at home), except:

  • Pete Wilkins played bass on `Hypocrite`.
  • Mike Ian played guitar on `Hypocrite`.
  • Dave Fecca played lead guitar on `Hypocrite` and slide guitar and dobro on `Can You Hear Me Now?`.
  • Cliff Hillis mixed `Nobody's There` at The Hacienda

Tom Volpicelli mastered everything at The Mastering House
